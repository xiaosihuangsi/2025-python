    8 Break & Continue Practice Demo of break to exit loops early and continue to skip iterations.
    9 Loop-Else Prime Checker Demonstrates the use of else with loops to identify prime numbers.
    10 Pass Statement Demo Demonstrates Python's pass statement as a placeholder in loops, classes, and functions.
    11 Match Statement Demo Demonstrates Python 3.10+ structural pattern matching.
